## Assembly Tips

### NRS Interface Board

**Tip 1:** Apply glue to secure connectors on the Interface Board. Use electronic assembly adhesive to prevent connector sockets from falling off or getting damaged due to frequent plugging and unplugging.

![Interface Board Connector Glue](img/interface-board-connector-glue.png)

**Tip 2:** Use KF2510 terminals and silicone wire to ensure current capacity above 3A.

### P-Series Hotend Base Mounting

**Tip 1:** Required parts: M3×16 hex screws, Head Diameter 8mm, Head Thickness 1mm (×2), PTFE washers 3×10×2 (×4), PTFE washers 3×6×2 (×4).

![P-Series Hotend Parts](img/p-hotend-parts.png)

**Tip 2:** Place the hotend base onto the mounting bracket. Insert M3×16 screws through the base holes and into the bracket.

![P-Series Hotend Assembly 1](img/p-hotend-assembly-1.png)

**Tip 3:** Insert PTFE washers onto the screws before securing. Place 3×10×2 washers on the outer positions and 3×6×2 washers on the inner positions.

![P-Series Hotend Assembly 2](img/p-hotend-assembly-2.png)

**Tip 4:** The PTFE washers are placed between the metal hotend base and the 3D printed frame to prevent heat transfer from the hotend to the printed model.

![P-Series Hotend Assembly 4](img/p-hotend-assembly-4.png)
